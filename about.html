Born in Canada and repping the DMV area, Nick Breezy (also known as "Kid Kente") is on the come up, looking to put his footprint on the hip-hop scene. The revolution will not be televised so you'd better get on the wave ASAP! Breezy is a proud member of the 1 Family 1 Foundation organization, a movement based in the Atlanta area, promoting positivity in inner-city communities and provindg great music from numerous artists. Nick is a resident of Atalanta himself and has performed in many venues around the country.
