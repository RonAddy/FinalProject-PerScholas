"1 Family 1 Foundation" is a non-profit organization determined to strengthen inner-city communities and promote hidden talent within them. Based out of Atlanta, and exhibiting strong roots from within the DMV, the 1Fam1Foundation movement looks to expand their outreach from beyond the east coast to a worldwide scope. Shown above are a few memebers of the board, as well as some of the artists who are part of this movement. To learn more or see a calendar of events, please visit the official website, or the Facebook and Instagram pages for live updates.
